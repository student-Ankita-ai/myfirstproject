Quantum Computing

Normal Computers are great at everyday tasks like browsing ,coding,games. But for very hard problems-like drug discovery,climate modeling,cracking huge codes,or optimzing thousands of routes-even supercomputers take years. But, QUANTUM COMPUTERS could solve it in mintues to hours.

Instead of checking one possibility at a time quantum computers explore millions of possibilities at once.


What is qubit?

Qubit is basic information unit of quantum computers like bit in normal computers but ,it can take multiple possibilities at the same time.

principles of Quantum Computing:

  1. Superpositions: qubit can hold many possible values at once.
  2. Entanglement : two qubits are linked ,changing one instantly affects the other (even if far apart)
  3. Interference : can "cancel out" wrong paths and strenghten correct ones ,likes tuning a radio.


Recent IIT Delhi and DRDO achievement

DRDO and IIT Delhi have collaborated ,through the DRDO- industry -academia centre of excellence (DIA-CoE) intiative ,to demonstrate a 1km free -space quantum secure communication link using quantum entanglement .This achievement ,announced in june 2025,represent a significant step for india into the "quantum era" of secure communications,offering fundamentally unbreakable encrypation for defence ,finance,and telecommunications.
